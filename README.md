# 📊 Sales Performance Analysis
An end-to-end data analytics project analyzing **$2.3M in retail sales** across 4 regions, 17 product sub-categories, and 4 years (2014–2017) using SQL, Python, and Power BI.

---

## Tools Used

**SQL** - Data extraction & aggregation \
**Python (Pandas)** - Data cleaning & analysis \
**Power BI** - Interactive dashboard & KPIs 

---

## What Was Done

**SQL** — Extracted regional sales, ranked top products, calculated monthly revenue, identified repeat customers, and computed average order values using aggregations and window functions.

**Python (Pandas)** — Cleaned and processed 9,994 records, computed moving averages, segmented customers into Low/Medium/High spenders, and identified top 10 customers by lifetime value.

**Power BI** — Built an interactive dashboard with revenue trend charts, product performance rankings, regional heatmap, category donut chart, customer segmentation, and 5 KPI cards — all cross-filtered by region and year slicers.

---

## Key Insights

- **$2.30M total revenue** across 5,009 orders from 793 customers
- **51% revenue growth** from 2014 ($484K) → 2017 ($733K)
- **98.5% repeat customer rate** — extremely high customer loyalty
- **West region leads** at $725K; South is lowest at $392K
- **Phones & Chairs** are top sub-categories (~$330K each)
- **Copiers have the highest margin** at 37.2%
- **Tables lose money** at -8.6% margin (-$17.7K loss)
- **Q4 drives 38% of annual revenue** — strong seasonality
